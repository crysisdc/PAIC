ABSTRACTS

As the only Paramedic conference in Australasia where abstracts are peer reviewed and attract a publication, again we will deliver cutting edge research from some of Australasia’s best Paramedic and pre-hospital researchers. In a developing profession and rapidly maturing subspecialty, abstract presentations encourage discussion amongst conferees, and provide inspiration and encouragement for budding researchers to find answers to the next big questions in our field.
